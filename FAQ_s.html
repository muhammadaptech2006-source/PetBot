Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, process, and services

Common Questions

Can't find an answer? Contact our team — we'd be happy to help.

What types of bottles do you manufacture?

We manufacture a comprehensive range of PET, PP, and PC bottles for diverse industries including packaged drinking water, pharmaceuticals, household chemicals, edible oils, liquor, hair care products, pesticides, and beverages. Sizes range from 50ml to 5,000ml. We also produce wide-mouth jars and specialty juice bottles.

Do you offer customization services?

Yes! We offer fully customizable bottles tailored to your brand specifications. Options include bottle size and capacity, shape and design, color (clear, amber, blue, green, or custom Pantone matching), neck finish types (PCO, ROPP, Crown, or custom), label area design, and embossing. Our team works closely with you from concept through production.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our minimum order quantity starts from 5,000 units for standard catalogue products. For custom mold development, MOQs may vary depending on product complexity. Please contact our sales team with your specific requirements and we'll find the most cost-effective solution for your business.

Are your bottles food-grade certified?

Yes, all our bottles are manufactured using food-grade materials and meet international quality standards. We hold ISO 9001:2015 certification, FDA compliance for food-grade materials, and international quality management certifications. Our in-house quality lab conducts 100% leak testing and rigorous in-process inspections on every batch.

What is the lead time for standard orders?

Standard orders from our existing product catalogue typically take 15–30 days depending on quantity and any minor customization. For custom mold development projects, an additional 30–45 days is required for mold fabrication and approval. We always confirm exact timelines at the time of order confirmation.

Do you provide samples before full production?

Absolutely. We provide pre-production samples for approval before committing to full runs, especially for custom orders. Sample charges may apply depending on the complexity of the design, but these are typically credited against your first production order. Contact us for details on our sample process.

Are your bottles environmentally friendly?

Yes. All our PET bottles are 100% recyclable and we actively encourage responsible disposal. Our manufacturing facility uses energy-efficient machinery and comprehensive waste recycling systems. We are committed to continuous improvement in our environmental footprint and hold relevant green manufacturing certifications.

Still have questions?

Our team of packaging experts is ready to guide you through the right solution for your specific needs.
